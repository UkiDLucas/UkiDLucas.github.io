Choice D

Since we are currently renting, we started looking at the houses we could afford.
This place fits our budget, but the baby blue color, "It's a boy!", does not inspire us.

Choice A

I grabbed a phone and, with a couple of sentences, described to the AI chat what we like.
The first proposal was too green for my wife, so we made it more brown. It is too dark brown, but we got the idea.

Choice B

Then I asked to render what I would like to make from the house, as I prefer Mediterranean villas.

Choice C

We might not get the house, or it might be 10 years before we change the exterior, but the point is that when dealing with an architect, we would wait for 2 weeks, and it would cost a ton of money.

I was able to do all of this 15 minutes after seeing the house, on the phone, in a restaurant, while waiting for dinner.

The point is that, with today's and tomorrow's tools, you can do 100 times more than you could a couple of years ago if you take the time to do it.
