# THIS Lowers Blood Sugar Levels FASTER than Anything Else


Dr. Eric Berg explains methods to lower blood sugar quickly and boost fat burning:

## 1. High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

Short bursts of intense exercise (like burpees, plyometrics, spin biking, or resistance training) quickly deplete excess blood sugar by forcing muscles to absorb glucose.

## 2. Apple Cider Vinegar

Acetic acid in apple cider vinegar can **lower post-meal blood sugar spikes by up to 34%**. A tablespoon in 8 oz of water after a high-carb meal improves glucose absorption into muscles.

## 3. Key Supplements

- [[Berberine]]: Natural alternative to metformin, effectively lowers blood sugar without side effects.
    
- [[NAC (N-acetyl cysteine)]]: Reduces oxidative stress linked to high blood sugar (600 mg recommended).
    
- [[Alpha-lipoic acid]]: Helps reverse nerve damage from high sugar levels.
    
- [[Vitamin B1 (Thiamine)]]: Often depleted after carb-heavy meals, leading to fat storage and post-sugar anxiety. Important to replenish after cheating on your diet.
    

## 4. Post-Meal Walking

A 25–30 minute walk after a carb-heavy meal burns excess glucose, preventing it from being stored as fat or cholesterol.

## 5. Potassium and Magnesium

- **[[Potassium]]**: Required for storing glucose in muscles and liver. Deficiency leads to insulin spikes.
    
- **[[Magnesium]]**: Critical for insulin-producing cells; most diabetics are deficient. Supplementing helps improve blood sugar control.
    

## 6. [[Ketone]] [[supplements]]

The brain can run on ketones instead of glucose, reducing its glucose demand. This supports better brain function, lowers inflammation, and helps prevent cognitive decline.

## 7. Salt Water

Adding sea salt to your last meal or drinking a small amount helps prevent stress-induced blood sugar spikes, promotes calmness, and improves sleep quality.

## 8. Dry Fasting (Most Powerful Method)

- **Dry fasting** (no food or water) reduces blood sugar faster than water fasting.
    
- It causes mild dehydration, concentrates blood sugar, and triggers hormonal responses that lower blood sugar and increase growth hormone.
    
- Dry fasting also enhances antioxidant production and promotes rapid fat burning by boosting ketone production.
    

## Final Advice

The best strategy is to **maintain a low-sugar diet consistently** to avoid needing these corrective measures.
