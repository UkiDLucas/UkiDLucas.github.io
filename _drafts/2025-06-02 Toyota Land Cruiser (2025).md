As a Jeep Wrangler owner and 4x4 enthusiast, here’s a clear breakdown of the 2025 Toyota 4Runner, Sequoia, Land Cruiser, and Lexus GX 550—four vehicles that share similar DNA but serve different purposes:

  

  

  

  

1. Toyota 4Runner (2025)

  

  

- Platform: TNGA-F (shared with Tacoma and Land Cruiser)
- Engine: 2.4L i-FORCE Turbo 4-cylinder (278 hp), with optional hybrid (~326 hp)
- Suspension: Solid rear axle, coil spring (better off-road articulation than IRS)
- Off-Road Gear: TRD Pro trim offers rear locker, Crawl Control, Multi-Terrain Select
- Size: Mid-size SUV (think slightly smaller than Land Cruiser)
- Focus: Classic off-roader, rock-crawling friendly, more analog feel
- Target Buyer: Practical 4x4 enthusiasts, overlanders, Jeep crowd alternative

  

  

  

  

  

2. Toyota Sequoia (2025)

  

  

- Platform: Same TNGA-F frame, but full-size (shared with Tundra)
- Engine: 3.4L twin-turbo V6 hybrid (437 hp, 583 lb-ft torque) standard
- Suspension: Independent rear suspension (better for comfort/towing, less flex)
- Off-Road Gear: Limited off-road chops; TRD Pro available, but more SUV than trail rig
- Size: Full-size 3-row SUV
- Focus: Power, towing, comfort—family road trips with occasional dirt road
- Target Buyer: Large families, trailer haulers, occasional adventurers

  

  

  

  

  

3. Toyota Land Cruiser (2025)

  

  

- Platform: TNGA-F (same as 4Runner, Tacoma)
- Engine: 2.4L i-FORCE MAX Hybrid (326 hp, 465 lb-ft torque)
- Suspension: Rear solid axle
- Off-Road Gear: Full-time 4WD, locking rear diff, Multi-Terrain Select, Crawl Control
- Size: Narrower and lighter than past Land Cruisers (close to 4Runner dimensions)
- Focus: Global overlander—rugged, reliable, efficient
- Target Buyer: Heritage-minded, long-distance explorers, former 80/100 series fans

  

  

  

  

  

4. Lexus GX 550 (2025)

  

  

- Platform: TNGA-F (shared with Land Cruiser)
- Engine: 3.4L twin-turbo V6 (349 hp, 479 lb-ft torque)
- Suspension: Solid rear axle, adaptive damping or KDSS-like system on higher trims
- Off-Road Gear: Full-time 4WD, optional e-locker, Multi-Terrain Monitor, Crawl Control
- Size: Similar to Land Cruiser, but more luxurious
- Focus: Off-road capability + premium cabin and technology
- Target Buyer: Upscale adventurers, overlanders who want comfort + capability
