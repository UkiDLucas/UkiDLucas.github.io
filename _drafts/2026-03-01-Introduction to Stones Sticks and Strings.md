Explanation:
"[[Seven Daughters of Eve 1]]" is the title of a book by [[Bryan Sykes]]. 
The book refers to the seven DNA lineages that can be traced back to prehistoric women, who lived in different parts of the world around 45,000 years ago. The dedication implies all of the past, present, and **future generations**.


The are multiple reasons why I started to write this book.

First, as a [[System Architect]] and a [[Software Manager]], I needed to keep notes on the full spectrum of [[Autonomous Technologies]]. 
In a given week, I could have multiple daily meetings ranging from [[sensor design]], [[calibration]], and [[perception]], to [[multi-sensor fusion]], [[road detection]], [[object detection]], [[multi-object tracking]],  [[path planning]] algorithms, [[vehicle controls]], [[chip design]], to [[Best Practices 1]], and governmental [standards compliance]. 

Second, after interviewing and hiring many candidates for the [[Autonomous Vehicles]] jobs, I realized how difficult it is to get into this field even for very talented [[software engineers]]. If this book helps even a couple of people, it will be a success.

The third reason was that I wanted to leave a legacy. My **family**, even my **wife**, and especially **my daughters** have no idea what I do at work. I wanted to create a book with an overview of my intellectual life. **This book is truly for you**. 

Finally, I was always wondering **how we, humans, got to be here**. 
I always wondered about pre-history, I loved to read biographies of discoverers and I wanted to understand the Why and the How and not just the What.
