I used to hate my e-mail.
Today, I look forward to opening it whenever I have a spare moment.

Below, I will show you how to make e-mail the most delightful, inspiring, educational, and thought-provoking experience.

The habit.
It became my habit to Google every author or speaker I was impressed with with the following query:

"Chris Williamson newsletter"

It changed my life!

I found out that:

“Anybody that matters has a newsletter.”

These days, I receive the carefully curated thoughts of the best minds of our time in my inbox.
They are not the attention-grabbing, knee-jerk reactions of social media trolls.
They are artfully crafted, beautiful thoughts that inspire, motivate, and expand your mind.

Uki D. Lucas
