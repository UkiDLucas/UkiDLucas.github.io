I have been reviewing the [State of AI 2023](https://www.stateof.ai/), and, as an effect, I wanted to share some of my thoughts.

There is no question that the multi-modality of Large Language Models (LLMs) such as ChatGPT is the topic of the day; text, images, and sounds are getting incorporated into the AI models with impressive results.

However, as someone working with 3D perception sensors, I suggest that the true revolution will happen when Machine Learning incorporates spatial thinking.

We already have excellent 3D modeling in games and simulations. Still, I believe that the actual floodgate opening will come with fleets of vehicles using various 3D sensors such as LiDARs and in combination with 2D sensors such as color and thermal cameras.
