I decided to write this open letter after an extensive set of interviews I have conducted in recent months, ranging from software interns, 
through developers, a principal architect, and even upper management.
In these interviews, I realized what I already knew but was suppressed because of the COVID-19 pandemic, moving to different states, and being busy at work.
The realization is that we all need mentorship.
You needed mentorship and apprenticeships in the old days because the information was unavailable. 
Today, with easy access to libraries, the Internet, and ChatGPT, you can have an AI mentor in any subject, whether it is a foreign language, leadership, philosophy, or technical skill.
