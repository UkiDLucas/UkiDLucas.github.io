### Detailed LiDAR Product Analysis:

### 1. Infinity Eye A (L4 Autonomous Driving):

- Configuration combines four **AT1440 ultra-high-definition lidars** and four **FTX solid-state lidars**.
    
- **Coverage:** Complete 360° with zero blind spots.
    
- **Applications:** Designed specifically for Robotaxis and Robotrucks, offering both short and long-range perception.
    

### AT1440 (Ultra-high-definition LiDAR used in Infinity Eye A):

- **Year of Production:** Mass production in H2 2025.
    
- **Channels:** 1,440 (highest automotive-grade LiDAR channel count globally at the time).
    
- **Maximum Range:** 300 meters @ 10% reflectivity.
    
- **Points per Second:** Exceeds 34 million points per second.
    
- **Resolution:** Delivers more than 45 times the point cloud density of typical automotive LiDARs​Hesai Unveils _Infinity….
    
- **Precision:** Can precisely perceive subtle road features such as small objects, bumps, and dips.
    

### FTX (Fully solid-state LiDAR used in Infinity Eye A & B):

- **Field of View (Horizontal x Vertical):** Up to 180° x 140°, with three horizontal FOV options: 120°, 140°, and 180°.
    
- **Resolution:** Double that of the previous generation.
    
- **Application:** Ideal for blind-spot detection, automated parking, and lane-change safety.
    

---

### 2. Infinity Eye B (L3 Conditional Autonomous Driving):

- Configuration includes one **ETX ultra-long-range LiDAR** and two **FTX solid-state LiDARs**.
    
- **Application:** Balances extended range detection with broad FOV for L3 conditions.
    

### ETX (Ultra-long-range LiDAR):

- **Maximum Range:** 400 meters @ 10% reflectivity.
    
- **Installation:** Compact design allows mounting behind windshield with a 32 mm sensor window.
    
- **Resolution:** Angular resolution of 0.05° (H) x 0.05° (V), 8 times the resolution of the AT128 LiDAR.
    
- **Year of Production:** Mass production by end of 2026.
    

---

### 3. Infinity Eye C (L2 Advanced Driver Assistance Systems):

- Utilizes the compact and powerful **ATX LiDAR**.
    
- **Maximum Range:** 200 meters @ 10% reflectivity.
    
- **Channels:** Up to 256.
    
- **Resolution:** Angular resolution of 0.1° x 0.1°.
    
- **Integration:** Flexible installation including behind windshield.
    
- **Noise Reduction:** Proprietary Intelligent Point Cloud Engine (IPE) filters 99.9% environmental noise.
